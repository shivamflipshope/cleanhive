Steam Ironing Process

    • Inspection: inspection of fabric type and condition to set the proper pressure and temperature
    • Prepping: The clothes are positioned, and the steam iron's water content, temperature, and heat are adjusted according to the fabric type to avoid damage.
    • Steam: High-pressure steam ironing is done delicately to remove wrinkles without damaging the fabric.

More Benefits

We all love crisp, wrinkle-free clothes, but the traditional ironing process doesn't give them that perfect finish. At CleanHive, our professionals use a high-pressure steam iron, which not only makes them wrinkle-free but also gives them character.

  • Is a steam iron safe for delicate fabrics?

    Yes. Steam ironing is safe for delicate fabrics, such as silk, chiffon, and cashmere. We adjust the heat and pressure based on the fabric type to avoid any type of damage to the fabric.

  • Will my clothes be damaged from the high-pressure steam iron?

    No. The high-pressure steam iron will not damage your clothes. A steam iron is safe, better, and gives more consistent and uniform results than a coal or electric iron.

  • Will a steam iron leave any iron mark on my clothes?

    No. Steam ironing does not leave any marks on your clothes, unlike other irons, such as coal or electric irons. The temperature in a steam iron can be adjusted according to the fabric's requirements. The result will be crisp, sharp clothes, free from wrinkles and marks.

  • What is the purpose of a steam iron?

    The steam ironing helps to relax the fabrics of your clothes, making it easier to smooth out wrinkles.

  • How long does the steam iron on clothes last?

    Depends on how you keep the clothes. If you keep the clothes neatly folded, then the clothes will remain wrinkle-free for days.
