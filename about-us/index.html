Our Mission

At CleanHive, our mission is to simplify laundry by offering easy booking, fast services, and making premium cleaning and dry cleaning services accessible to everyone. Pankaj Kumar

Founder & CEO
CleanHive was founded by Pankaj, driven by his passion for entrepreneurship and 'customer first' mindset, he always had a vision to transform people's laundry experience into something better.
