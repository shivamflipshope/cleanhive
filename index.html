    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What services does CleanHive offer?

      We offer various services, such as laundry, dry cleaning, shoe cleaning, a high-pressure steam iron, and pick-up and drop-off services.

    • Are my clothes safe if I choose online dry cleaners?

      Yes, definitely! Your clothes are safe with us because we use organic detergents and wash them gently. Moreover, our cleaners are highly professional and use correct washing measures.

    • What kind of payment methods does CleanHive accept?

      We accept payment methods like UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery.

    • How do I place an offer here?

      After visiting our website, you can add your address, select the laundry service you want with the desired time slot, and our team will do the rest.

    • What happens if there is any issue with my order?

      You can connect with us via phone or email regarding your issue with our customer care support system.

    • How much time does CleanHive take to deliver the orders?

      We usually take at least 3 days to clean your clothes according to your requirements, but we also have a facility for providing fast/ express /urgent service.

    • Will there be any odour in my clothes from the solvents?

      No. The solvent will not leave any foul odour in your clothes; we use organic solvents that make your clothes smell fresh.

    • How will I be notified when my clothes are ready?

      We will update you via a call or a message whenever your clothes are out for delivery.

    • Can you clean leather, suede, fur, or delicate fabrics like silk?

      Yes! At CleanHive, we gently clean sensitive fabrics like leather, suede, fur, or silk.

    • Do you provide a shoe cleaning service?

      Yes. We offer shoe cleaning services for any stubborn stain on any type of shoe, be it canvas, suede, or leather.

    • Will there be any discolouration in my clothing after dry cleaning?

      No. Our cleaning services will not discolour your clothes or damage them.

    • Can I schedule or reschedule when I want my clothes delivered?

      Yes. You can contact our store and schedule a date and time for when you want your clothes delivered, according to your convenience.

    • Do I need to sort my clothes before handing them to you?

      You don't need to sort your clothes before handing them to us. We will sort your clothes based on fabrics and cleaning processes. You can provide specific care instructions for any special item or fabric.

    • What is the cost of cleaning services at CleanHive?

      The cost of cleaning services at CleanHive depends on factors such as the type of fabric, the extent of cleaning required, and any additional services requested. Please contact us or visit our website for detailed pricing information.

    • What if my clothes get messed up or crumpled while delivering?

      No. We deliver your clothes with utmost care and sincerity, and our professional services and packaging prevent them from getting crumpled.

    • Can you clean stubborn stains like oil, wine, or ink?

      Yes. We can clean harsh stains from oil, wine, and ink. Our organic solvents and cleaning process thoroughly clean your clothes.

    • Can dry cleaning shrink or stretch my clothes?

      No. Our dry cleaning services do not shrink or stretch your clothes.

    • How do I know if my clothes need washing or dry cleaning?

      You must know the fabric type to decide whether your clothes need washing or dry cleaning. You can also check the care label instructions tagged with your clothes and the type of stain your clothes have.

    • How do you ensure my clothes will not be lost or mixed with someone elses?

      When your clothes arrive at our cleaning centre, we tag them so they don't get mixed up or lost.
